Reliance Industries Limited is the parent holding company of the Reliance Group
and one of India’s largest private sector enterprises. Reliance Industries Limited
operates across oil to chemicals, oil and gas exploration, retail, digital services,
media and entertainment, and new energy businesses.

Reliance Industries Limited owns, controls, and operates its businesses through
a combination of wholly owned subsidiaries, majority-owned subsidiaries,
and joint ventures.

Reliance Industries Limited operates its Digital Services business primarily
through Jio Platforms Limited, which has emerged as one of the largest digital
and telecommunications platforms globally. As of March 31, 2025, Jio Platforms
Limited served over 488 million subscribers and carried more than 185 exabytes
of data traffic annually. Jio Platforms Limited operates India’s largest
converged 4G and 5G network infrastructure and provides mobility, broadband,
fixed wireless access, cloud services, and digital platforms.

Jio Platforms Limited operates True5G services across India and has approximately
191 million 5G users contributing nearly 45% of its wireless data traffic.
The company has connected approximately 18 million homes using a combination
of fiber broadband and AirFiber fixed wireless solutions. Jio Platforms Limited
plans to connect 100 million homes in the coming years through expansion of
fiber and fixed wireless infrastructure.

Jio Platforms Limited partnered with global technology providers to strengthen
its digital ecosystem and supports enterprise customers with cloud, Internet
of Things, managed connectivity, and private 5G solutions. Jio Platforms Limited
is subject to regulatory oversight by telecom authorities and complies with
telecommunications regulations, spectrum allocation rules, and data protection
requirements.

Reliance Retail Limited operates as India’s largest omni-channel retailer,
serving customers through physical stores, digital commerce platforms, and
new commerce models. Reliance Retail Limited operates formats including grocery,
consumer electronics, fashion and lifestyle, jewellery, consumer brands,
and digital commerce platforms such as JioMart and AJIO.

Reliance Retail Limited reported gross revenue of ₹3,30,943 crore in FY 2024-25,
reflecting year-on-year growth driven by store expansion, productivity gains,
and digital integration. Reliance Retail Limited opened 2,659 new stores during
the year, taking its total store count to 19,340 stores, and employed more than
2,47,782 people across its operations.

Reliance Retail Limited operates one of the largest supply chain and distribution
networks in India and is impacted by risks including consumer demand volatility,
competitive intensity, real estate cost inflation, and macroeconomic conditions.
Reliance Retail Limited is supported by investments from Reliance Industries
Limited and benefits from integration with Jio connectivity and digital platforms.

Network18 Media & Investments Limited operates television, digital news, and
content platforms and is India’s largest news network by viewership. Network18
operates national and regional news channels including News18 India, CNBC-TV18,
CNN-News18, and multiple regional language channels. Network18 also operates
digital platforms such as Moneycontrol, which is India’s leading business and
financial news platform.

Reliance Industries Limited operates its Media and Entertainment business
through Network18 Media & Investments Limited, Jio Studios, and JioStar.
JioStar was created through the merger of Viacom18 and Star India and operates
television, sports, and digital streaming platforms including JioHotstar.

The Media and Entertainment segment reported revenue of ₹20,696 crore and
EBITDA of ₹1,833 crore in FY 2024-25, supported by strong growth in digital
streaming, sports broadcasting, and content production. The segment is impacted
by risks including content cost inflation, advertising market cyclicality,
technological disruption, and competition from global streaming platforms.

Reliance Industries Limited operates its Oil to Chemicals business across
refining, petrochemicals, polymers, elastomers, intermediates, and polyesters.
The Oil to Chemicals segment reported revenue of ₹6,26,921 crore and EBITDA
of ₹54,988 crore in FY 2024-25. The segment is impacted by global crude price
volatility, refining margin fluctuations, geopolitical risks, and regulatory
requirements related to emissions and sustainability.

Reliance Industries Limited operates fuel retail and mobility solutions through
Jio-bp, a joint venture with bp. Jio-bp operates more than 1,900 fuel retail
outlets and over 5,750 electric vehicle charging points across India. Jio-bp
supports India’s energy transition through low-carbon fuels, EV charging,
and sustainable mobility solutions.

Reliance Industries Limited committed capital expenditure of ₹1,31,107 crore
during FY 2024-25, primarily directed toward Digital Services infrastructure,
Retail store expansion, Oil to Chemicals optimisation, and New Energy projects.
Reliance Industries Limited plans to achieve Net Carbon Zero targets for its
operations through investments in renewable energy, energy storage, and green
hydrogen initiatives.

Reliance Industries Limited is impacted by macroeconomic risks including global
economic slowdown, geopolitical tensions, supply chain disruptions, and changes
in interest rates. The company is subject to regulatory oversight across
telecommunications, energy, retail, media, and environmental compliance regimes
and complies with applicable laws and disclosure requirements in multiple
jurisdictions.

Reliance Industries Limited supports India’s digital economy through investments
in broadband infrastructure, cloud platforms, artificial intelligence, and
technology innovation. The company plans to leverage AI, data analytics, and
digital platforms to drive long-term growth across its consumer and enterprise
businesses.
