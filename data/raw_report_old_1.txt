Reliance Industries Limited operates across energy, petrochemicals, retail and digital services. 
In FY 2023-24, Reliance recorded revenues of ₹10,00,122 crore (US$ 119.9 billion) 
with a profit after tax of ₹79,020 crore (US$ 9.5 billion). 
The company reported that its retail subsidiary, Reliance Retail Ventures Limited, 
continued its growth momentum, while Jio Platforms Limited maintained 
strong digital services performance. Significant risk factors 
include regulatory compliance challenges, foreign exchange volatility, 
and market competition. Reliance Retail owns Hamleys India Private Limited.
