Reliance Industries Limited is the parent holding company of the Reliance Group
and one of India’s largest private sector enterprises. Reliance Industries Limited
operates across oil to chemicals, oil and gas exploration, retail, digital services,
media and entertainment, and new energy businesses.

Reliance Industries Limited owns, controls, and operates its businesses through
a combination of wholly owned subsidiaries, majority-owned subsidiaries,
and joint ventures.

Reliance Retail Limited is a subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited and operates
a nationwide retail network across grocery, consumer electronics, fashion,
lifestyle, and e-commerce formats. Reliance Retail Limited continues to expand
its physical store footprint and omni-channel capabilities across India.

Jio Platforms Limited is a subsidiary of Reliance Industries Limited and operates
digital services, telecommunications infrastructure, and technology platforms.
Jio Platforms Limited provides mobility, broadband, digital applications,
and cloud-based services to consumers and enterprises.

Network18 Media & Investments Limited is part of the Reliance Group and operates
media and entertainment businesses across television, digital platforms,
and content production.

Jio-bp is a fuel retail and mobility joint venture between Reliance Industries Limited
and bp. Jio-bp operates fuel retail outlets, EV charging infrastructure, and
energy transition solutions across India. Reliance Industries Limited does not
own bp, and Jio-bp does not own Reliance Industries Limited.

Reliance Industries Limited partnered with bp to form Jio-bp as a joint venture
focused on fuel retailing, mobility solutions, and low-carbon energy initiatives.

Reliance Industries Limited reported consolidated revenue of ₹10,71,174 crore
for the financial year FY 2024-25. EBITDA for the year was ₹1,83,422 crore.
Net profit attributable to shareholders stood at ₹81,309 crore.

Capital expenditure during FY 2024-25 amounted to ₹1,31,000 crore and was
primarily directed toward digital services expansion, retail growth,
petrochemical optimisation, and energy transition initiatives.

Reliance Retail Limited reported strong revenue growth supported by store
expansion, increased customer footfalls, and digital integration.
Jio Platforms Limited reported growth in subscribers and data consumption,
contributing significantly to consolidated financial performance.

Reliance Industries Limited raised capital through internal accruals and
market instruments to support long-term growth initiatives.
Reliance Industries Limited invested in digital platforms, retail infrastructure,
and renewable energy technologies.

Reliance Industries Limited faces multiple risk factors that may impact its
operations, financial performance, and strategic objectives.

Key risks include market volatility affecting consumer demand and margins.
Geopolitical uncertainties impacting global energy markets and supply chains.
Regulatory and policy changes affecting telecommunications, energy,
and retail businesses.

Competitive intensity across digital services, retail, and energy segments
may impact pricing, margins, and market share.
Execution risks related to large-scale capital expenditure projects
and technology transformation initiatives may affect timelines and returns.

jurisdictions and complies with applicable laws, regulations, and disclosure
requirements.
